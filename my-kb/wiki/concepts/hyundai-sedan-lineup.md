# Hyundai Sedan Lineup

## Overview
The KB presents Hyundai sedans as a layered lineup. Avante represents the compact, practical, high-volume side of Hyundai's sedan strategy, while Grandeur represents the upper-tier, comfort-focused, status-oriented side.

## Avante
Avante AD and CN7 show the development of Hyundai's compact sedan strategy. AD is framed around improved basics, stronger product completion, and practical value. CN7 extends the same nameplate with bolder design, more digital features, stronger safety content, hybrid options, and N/N Line performance derivatives.

## Grandeur
Grandeur sits above Sonata and Avante as Hyundai's long-running flagship sedan nameplate. Its meaning changed over time: early generations symbolized domestic luxury, while current generations function as Hyundai's top mainstream sedan after Genesis became a separate premium brand.

## Lineup Meaning
Together, these documents show Hyundai using sedans for different market roles:
- Avante: practical compact sedan, efficiency, youth and family use, performance derivatives
- Grandeur: executive image, family comfort, corporate use, 2-row comfort, high domestic status
- Hyundai Motor Company: the broader brand and manufacturing network behind both
