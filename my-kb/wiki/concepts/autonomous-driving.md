# Autonomous Driving

## Overview
The Hyundai Motor summary references autonomous-driving and connected-car investment as part of the company's technology strategy. The Avante CN7 summaries show this strategy at the model level through expanded driver-assistance systems.

## Corporate Strategy
Hyundai is described as investing in autonomous driving and connected systems alongside electrification and broad powertrain development. This positions automation as part of a larger technical modernization effort.

## Model-Level Evidence
The CN7 summaries mention safety and driver-assistance features such as forward collision avoidance, lane keeping, lane departure prevention, driver attention warning, high-beam assist, side monitoring, and related warning or warranty issues.

These features are not full autonomy, but they show how Hyundai deploys automation-like functions into volume models.
