# Hybrid And Performance Models

## Overview
Hybrid and performance derivatives appear across Hyundai's sedan strategy. They let mainstream models cover efficiency, sportiness, and premium use cases without becoming entirely separate vehicle lines.

## Hybrid Direction
Hyundai Motor's broader powertrain strategy includes hybrid, EV, hydrogen, LPG, diesel, and gasoline coverage. At the model level, Avante CN7 includes a 1.6 hybrid derivative, while Grandeur has a continuing HEV line across HG, IG, and GN7 generations.

## Performance Direction
The Avante summaries show a progression from AD Sport to CN7 N Line and N. This reflects a shift from a single sporty derivative toward Hyundai's more formal N performance structure.

## Strategic Meaning
These derivatives expand the same sedan nameplate into different buyer needs:
- hybrids for efficiency and refined daily use
- N Line for sporty appearance and feel
- N for high-performance identity
- Grandeur hybrid for comfort-oriented upper-tier efficiency
