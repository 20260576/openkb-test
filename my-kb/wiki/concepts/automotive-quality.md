# Automotive Quality

## Overview
Automotive quality is a cross-document theme in this KB. Hyundai Motor is described as having improved dramatically from early export-era criticism, but individual model summaries still record defects, recalls, and service campaigns.

## Company-Level Quality Narrative
The Hyundai Motor summary frames quality as a long-term reputation arc:
- early reliability criticism
- warranty and engineering responses
- improved initial-quality results
- continued skepticism around durability and defect response

This gives Hyundai's quality story a mixed character: improvement is real, but not complete.

## Model-Level Issues
The Avante AD summaries mention issues such as engine-oil increase on some Sport models, battery-fluid vapor concerns, component assembly defects, and some 2.0 MPI engine recalls.

The Avante CN7 summaries mention side radar setting errors, early battery discharge, air-conditioning noise, under-cover fixing issues, brake warning lamps, warranty extension for front safety-system warnings, and wireless charging logic correction.

## Overall Meaning
The KB treats quality not as a single rating but as a practical ownership topic. Hyundai's brand rehabilitation depends on engineering progress, but ownership experience still includes recalls, service campaigns, and reliability perception.
