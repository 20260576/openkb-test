# Electric Vehicles

## Overview
The Hyundai Motor summary describes electrification as part of the company's broad technology coverage. Hyundai is not presented as only a gasoline automaker; its product strategy includes hybrid, electric, and hydrogen fuel-cell vehicles.

## Product Scope
The lineup summary mentions electrified models such as Ioniq 5, Ioniq 6, Ioniq 9, and fuel-cell products such as Nexo. This places EVs inside a wider Hyundai strategy that also includes internal-combustion cars, LPG vehicles, hybrids, commercial vehicles, and hydrogen systems.

## Strategic Meaning
Electrification appears as one way Hyundai strengthens its global competitiveness and updates its brand identity. In this KB, EVs connect to Hyundai's engineering breadth, global manufacturing scale, and attempt to move from follower status toward more advanced technology leadership.
