# Motorsport Marketing

## Overview
Hyundai's motorsport activity is part of its broader brand-building strategy. The company summary mentions WRC, customer racing, Nürburgring endurance activity, and future Genesis-linked prototype racing.

## Performance Branding
The Avante CN7 summaries connect this corporate performance direction to production models through N Line and N. The earlier Avante Sport in the AD generation becomes a bridge toward the CN7-era N structure.

## Marketing Role
Motorsport gives Hyundai a way to demonstrate engineering credibility beyond ordinary passenger cars. In the KB, this matters because Hyundai's brand story includes overcoming older quality perceptions and developing a more premium, performance-capable image.
